# Design System Document

This document outlines the core aesthetic and functional principles of our user interface, derived from our design tokens.

## Theme Overview

Our theme aims for a **light** appearance, offering a clear and accessible user experience. The overall tone is established with a subtle and muted aesthetic.

## Color Palette

The color scheme is meticulously selected to ensure brand consistency and visual hierarchy.

-   **Primary Color**: `#70797c` - A deep, grounding gray-blue, serving as the foundational color for key interactive elements and brand accents.
-   **Secondary Color**: `#757779` - A supporting mid-tone gray, ideal for less prominent UI elements, subtle dividers, and secondary actions.
-   **Tertiary Color**: `#637a8d` - An accentuating gray-blue, used for highlights, badges, or decorative elements to add depth without overpowering.
-   **Neutral Color**: `#777777` - A versatile and balanced gray, forming the base for backgrounds, surfaces, and non-chromatic components.

## Typography

Our typographic choices are designed for readability and aesthetic appeal, establishing a clear visual hierarchy.

### Headlines

-   **Font Family**: Public Sans
-   **Purpose**: Used for all major headings, ensuring a modern and impactful presence.

### Body Text

-   **Font Family**: Public Sans
-   **Purpose**: Applied to all paragraphs and longer texts, chosen for its high readability across various screen sizes.

### Labels & UI Elements

-   **Font Family**: Public Sans
-   **Purpose**: Utilized for labels, buttons, and other UI components, ensuring consistency with body text for a harmonious interface.

## Shape and Form

The geometry of our UI elements contributes to the overall feel and interaction.

-   **Roundedness**: `1` (Subtle roundedness) - Elements feature a hint of roundedness at their corners, offering a soft yet defined appearance.

## Spacing

The approach to spacing influences the perceived density and breathability of the interface.

-   **Spacing**: `2` (Normal) - A balanced approach to whitespace, providing comfortable separation between elements without feeling sparse or cramped.
